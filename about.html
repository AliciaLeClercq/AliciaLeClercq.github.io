About Us

Our Story

At Wild Flower, we specialize in offering a distinctive and curated selection of pressed flowers. Each blossom is meticulously handpicked and expertly pressed to preserve its natural beauty and vibrant colors.

Our pressed flowers are perfect for enhancing your home decor, creating memorable gifts, or adding a personal touch to your letters and journaling projects. Discover the timeless elegance of nature with Wild Flower.

Our Process

Learn how we press your flowers to timeless pieces!

Select Blooms

We choose fresh, vibrant flowers of your choice that have recently bloomed. We then arrange the flowers on one sheet of parchment paper.

Pressing

We place the second piece of paper on top of arranged flowers. We make sure to flatten the flower faces in the shape you'd like to achieve when pressed.

Arrangement

We wait anywhere between 2-4 weeks for the flowers to dry. This is dependent on the thickness of the petals. Once pressed, we then arrange them into the design you picked!
